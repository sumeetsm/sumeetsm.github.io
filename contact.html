Contact me

If you like working on light alloys with focus on metal plasticity, deformation kinetics, phenomenological modelling, crystallographic texture and precipitation modelling, then please feel free to contact me here. The different research topics will involve an ensemble of various experimental and modelling tools which in turn will help you prepare for your professional career moving forward. All the research topics will provide you with a unique opportunity of developing either an experimental tool or a modelling strategy which in turn will brush up your innovative skills. We believe that imagination, improvisation and innovative skills are the key requirements for succeeding in the competitive global world. We will strive to ensure that your stay in our team is an enjoyable experience and we look forward to developing an efficient partnership with you to achieve the research goals.
